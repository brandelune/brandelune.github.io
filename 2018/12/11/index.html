2018/12/11, thirty-first day in a row

A month already

The beginning is already shrouded in the past. I faintly remember when I started and what I did then, but only reading the daily notices manages to bring back the memories.

So, what have I learned in a month?

I'll try to keep the list short and general.

  • It's easy to loose track of what you want to accomplish.
  • You tend to try shortcuts and then you get lost.
  • It is hard to go back on track.
  • It is good to find similarly minded people. But it is hard to find them.
  • And they deal with the same issues, so it's not easy to go forward together.
  • Maybe learning on your own is hard because it gets awfully lonely down here.
